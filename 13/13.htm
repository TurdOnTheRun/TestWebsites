by James Fleckenstein

Last Updated: 2015-05-18 02:04:26

A browser based game - you, the player, must increase the size of your cell within a white realm full of small cells, and enemy player cells as a MMO. Agar.io is an online game which was first posted on 4chan on April 28, 2015, immediately becoming a very popular game overnight. With in a week it was added to Steam Greenlight and was approved right away. Agar io game was published and the domain was bought on the same day the game was posted. 


Agar io Free Download

In this Agar.io free download you will have direct access to the game within your browser in minutes. This Agar.io browser game's objective is to grow as large as possible by engulfing cell pellets and enemy cells. The enemy cells are real people online playing against you and trying to grow as large as possible to engulf you and everyone else. You can split into smaller cells by pressing the space bar, or you can collide with the viruses. You can also shoot other players by pressing W, which shoots parts of your cell at enemy players. 

If you want to have more fun and add personality to your cell, you can add skins to your cell / blob by selecting hidden keywords as your nickname. A few of these nicknames include Doge, Wojak, 4chan, 8chan, and 9gag. This makes the game even more crazy by adding crazy Agar.io skins to your cell, and you will see other cells with skins as other people discover the hidden keywords as well.
