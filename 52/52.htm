Deck the halls with Caitlyn Jenner!

The “I Am Cait” star made a show-stopping appearance at her ex Kris Jenner’s annual Christmas Eve party on Thursday night.

Caitlyn, 66, stunned in a floor-length black evening gown that let the former Olympian show a lot of leg.

The reality star attended the event one day after she revealed that her ex-wife’s Christmas Eve party was her favorite holiday tradition.

"Probably the biggest tradition I have along with the rest of the family, is Kris,” Caitlyn said in a video message on her website Wednesday. “For the last 30 years her Christmas Eve party is over the top spectacular."

"She does such a beautiful job with decorating the house. We've had the same Santa Claus for 25 years."


Kris' party was an All-Star affair, according to social media photos and videos taken at the soiree.

Drake, Jennifer Lopez, Toni Braxton and Hailey Baldwin were on hand for the momager’s annual blowout, as were Kim, Kourtney and Khloe Kardashian and Kylie and Kendall Jenner.


Kim, who has been lying low since giving birth to her second child Saint earlier this month, made a rare public appearance at the party, and attended with daughter North.

Kylie got into the holiday spirit by coordinating her fashion with her mom, with both wearing similar Balmain mini-dresses to the shindig.


View Gallery Kris Jenner’s annual Christmas Eve party

The 18-year-old's boyfriend Tyga also attended the party, but based on social media pics, it is unclear whether Kim's husband Kanye West and Kourtney’s ex-partner Scott Disick were also at the fete.

The Kardashian-Jenner clan kicked off holiday celebrations earlier this week with the debut of the family's 2015 Christmas card, featuring North and her cousins Mason, Penelope and Reign Disick.
