Caitlyn Jenner Steps Out After Spending Christmas with the Kardashians

7SHARES
Reprint
78 Comments subscribe now

By Michele Corriston

@mcorriston

12/29/2015 AT 09:10 AM EST

Caitlyn Jenner's holiday style game is still going strong.

The reality star stepped out in a chic winter-white shirt, jeans and strappy heels Monday to grab coffee in Malibu, California.

Caitlyn Jenner

x17

Caitlyn, 66, spent Christmas with the Kardashians and daughters Kendall and Kylie Jenner, donning a festive snowman onesie in a video posted to stepdaughter Kourtney Kardashian's website Monday.

Caitlyn Jenner in a holiday onesie

Kourtney Kardashian

The Olympic legend even earned a stocking embroidered with her name at ex-wife Kris Jenner's Calabasas mansion, where the momager hosted her annual Christmas Eve bash.

Keep up with your favorite celebs in the pages of PEOPLE Magazine by subscribing now.


The Kardashian-Jenner family's stockings

Kendall Jenner Instagram

"Probably the biggest tradition I have along with the rest of the family, is Kris for the last 30 years her Christmas Eve party is over-the-top spectacular," Caitlyn said in during a fan Q&A on Wednesday. "She does such a beautiful job with decorating the house. We've had the same Santa Claus for 25 years."

"It's a great time for our family, a great time for all our close friends," she added. "It just really kicks off our holiday season perfectly, and it certainly is a lot of fun."
